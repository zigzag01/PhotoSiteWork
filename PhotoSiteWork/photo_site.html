Here are the FAQs!


Here you can find answers to the most frequently asked questions!


  • How long does the training last?

    Background provides three types of courses:
    BASIC - 3 months
    LIGHT - 2 months
    SPECIAL - 1 month
    For questions about the specifics of each course, write to the contact phone number on our website.

  • What do I need to complete the photo retouching course?

    A computer with Photoshop and Lightroom installed in Russian. You can get started with a mouse, but in the future I recommend buying a graphics tablet, retouching will be much more convenient with it.

  • Does Background provide photo studios?

    Background cooperates with other photostudios in our city, which are always ready to provide a studio. For all questions, write to the contact number on our website.

  • What is the maximum period of cooperation with a certain photographer?

    Each term of cooperation is discussed with the administrator and the photographer individually. You can contact the photographer through the portfolio on our website.

  • Is there an online course for applicants from other localities?

    Unfortunately, at this stage of development of our studio, online trainings are not provided :(
    We advise you to follow our updates and be aware of the changes.

  • What kind of camera is needed for training on the course?

    A mirrorless or mirrorless camera of amateur level or higher is suitable for training. If you don't have it yet, you will be able to make a choice at the start of the course and we will tell you what characteristics you need to pay attention to when buying.
